//History of programming:

Ada Lovelace & Charles Babbage

it's all started when Ada Lovelace the English mathematician met another mathematician called Charles Babbage who had the idea of designing a calculating machine called the difference engine. Lovelace admired the idea of the different engine and worked with Babbage on his new idea which is a more advanced machine called the analytical engine In 1843 Lovelace translated a French paper that Italian mathematician Luigi Menabrea wrote about the Analytical Engine she added thousands of words to the paper. Lovelace believed that the analytical engine will produce a lot of potential by carrying out a massive number of mathematical operations. The example about what she wrote was how to calculate Bernoulli numbers

Alan Turing

Alan Turing is the founder of modern computer science by inventing "the automatic computing engine" which is the first complete specification of an electronic stored-program all-purpose digital computer. if it's built as he planned it would have had vastly more memory than any of the other early computers as well as being faster. however the NPL (national physics laboratory) thought the engineering is too difficult to be applied and a smaller machine was built, the pilot model ACE in 1950

Grace hopper

grace hopper was a Ph.D. in mathematics, who devoted almost her entire life to computers and programming. and she has built the first compiler in 1952 (for Sperry rand computer) known as the a-o system and she said that she invented the compiler because she was lazy and she hoped that the programmer return to be a mathematician. she published The Education of a Computer, in which she described fundamental principles in programming and anticipated future developments.

The Ada language

ada language is a high-level programming language that was originally designed by a team led by French computer scientist Jean Ichbiah of CII Honeywell Bull under contract to the United States Department of Defense (DoD) from 1977 to 1983 but The first appearance was in February 1980.
